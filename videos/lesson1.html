Canva Video Editing

Complete Practical Lesson for Beginners

Welcome

Welcome to today's practical class on Video Editing with Canva. This lesson is completely practical. By following each step, you will create a real video from beginning to end.

Lesson Objective

At the end of this lesson, every student should be able to create a complete video containing:

  • 🎥 Video
  • 🖼️ Picture
  • ✍️ Text
  • 🎵 Background Music
  • 🎤 Voice Recording (Optional)
  • ✨ Animation
  • 🔄 Transition
  • 📥 Export the finished video
STEP 1

Open Canva

  1. Open the Canva App or Canva Website.
  2. Sign in to your account.
  3. Click Create a Design.
  4. Select Mobile Video (1080 × 1920).
Note:

Every page in Canva represents one scene in your video.
STEP 2

Add a Video

  1. Click Uploads.
  2. Upload a short video from your phone or computer.
  3. Drag the video onto the page.
  4. Resize it to fill the screen.
  5. Trim the video by dragging either end of the timeline.
Practice: Trim a 20-second video down to about 8 seconds.
STEP 3

Add a Picture

  1. Create another page.
  2. Upload a picture.
  3. Drag it onto the page.
  4. Resize it.
  5. Move it anywhere you like.
Pictures help tell your story just like videos.
STEP 4

Add Text

Text helps explain your video and keeps your audience engaged.

  1. Click Text.
  2. Select Add a Heading.
  3. Type:

Welcome to My First Canva Video

Now Customize Your Text

  • Change the font style.
  • Increase or decrease the font size.
  • Change the text colour.
  • Make the text bold.
  • Move the text to any position on the page.
STEP 5

Animate Your Text

Animations make your text look more attractive.

  1. Select the text.
  2. Click Animate.
  3. Choose any animation you like.

Examples

  • Fade
  • Rise
  • Pop
  • Typewriter
  • Breathe
  • Drift
Click the Play button to preview the animation.
STEP 6

Add Background Music

  1. Click Audio.
  2. Search for any music.

Suggested Search Words

  • Happy
  • Piano
  • Inspiration
  • Motivation
  • Corporate
  • Travel

After Selecting Music

  • Reduce the volume.
  • Trim the music.
  • Move where the music starts.
  • Play the video to hear the result.
STEP 7

Record Your Voice (Optional)

If your Canva version supports recording, you can add your own narration.

  1. Open Uploads.
  2. Choose Record Yourself or Record Voice.
  3. Record your voice.
  4. Save the recording.
  5. Add it to your project.
Practice Voice Script

"Welcome everyone. Today we are learning Canva video editing. By the end of this lesson, you will create your own professional-looking video."

STEP 8

Add Elements

Elements make your videos more interesting.

  1. Click Elements.
  2. Search for any object.

Try Searching For

  • Arrow
  • Star
  • Circle
  • Fire
  • Heart
  • Check Mark
  • Line
  • Glow

Practice

  • Insert an element.
  • Resize it.
  • Rotate it.
  • Move it around.
  • Animate it.
Do not overcrowd your page with too many elements. Use only those that support your message.
STEP 9

Add Page Transitions

Transitions create smooth movement between pages.

  1. Click the small + between two pages.
  2. Select Add Transition.

Popular Transitions

  • Dissolve
  • Slide
  • Circle Wipe
  • Match & Move
  • Flow
  • Pan
Preview each transition before choosing your favourite one.
STEP 10

Adjust Page Timing

Every page in your video should stay on the screen long enough for viewers to read the text comfortably.

  1. Select a page.
  2. Click the page duration (time).
  3. Set the timing to any of the following:
  • 5 Seconds
  • 8 Seconds
  • 10 Seconds
Experiment with different timings and choose the one that looks best.
STEP 11

Preview Your Video

Before downloading your work, always preview it.

  1. Click the Play button.
  2. Watch the entire video.
  3. Check for mistakes.

Things to Check

  • Is the text easy to read?
  • Is the music too loud?
  • Are the animations smooth?
  • Are the transitions working properly?
  • Does everything look neat?
A good video editor always previews the project before exporting.
STEP 12

Export Your Video

  1. Click Share.
  2. Select Download.
  3. Choose MP4 Video.
  4. Click Download.
  5. Wait for Canva to finish exporting.
🎉 Congratulations! Your video is now ready to share with others.
